The Land of Innovation


MAARS LAB VISION



Research and Develop Engineered Systems and Agricultural Technologies for Climate-Smart Agricultural Practices.

scroll down

MISSION

  • Develop Artificial Intelligence (AI)-enabled ground and aerial robotic technologies for precise management of crop inputs with minimal negative impacts to environment.
  • Develop intelligence for field-scalable ground and aerial robotic swarms.
  • Develop and deploy farmer-friendly decision support tools for improved profitability and climate resilience.
  • Foster and nurture professional development of students and scholars to help identify and advance their career pathways.
  • Train the next-generation workforce to work at the intersection of agriculture, engineered systems, robotics, AI, and natural resource management.
  • Contribute to the mission of convergent research through interdisciplinary collaborations across multiple partner groups (e.g., other engineering programs, non-engineering programs, producers, equipment manufacturers, policy makers, and NGOs).

VALUES

  • Every person and every interaction matters.
  • Create a welcoming, thriving, and inclusive environment.
  • A place that values diversity, equity, and inclusion.
  • Take pride in individual and team outputs, with a focus on teamwork.
  • Build and value connections across both academia and industry.
  • Encourage local and global societal impact by creating an inspiring and creative environment.
